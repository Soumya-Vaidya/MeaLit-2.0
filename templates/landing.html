Our Journey

MeaLit began its journey as a food-based web-app. It started off as a simple C++ based food ordering system as a Semester Project. We eventually built on the idea of food ordering and delivery system as another project for following semester. Eventually, after lot of market analysis and surveys, we realized that what people really need is a all-in-one platform for all their food needs, not just another food-delivery app.

Thus, came the idea of MeaLit 2.0! MeaLit 2.0 is revamped version of MeaLit 1.0 which provides recommendations to its users, whether it be for the restaurants they like to hang out or whip something up in their own kitchen!
